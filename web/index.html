Master the Commands.

All commands are available as both slash commands and prefix commands.

CHARACTER MANAGEMENT
/importsheet <url>
Link your Google Sheet (SWN, WWN, or CWN) for automated stats and sync.
/importjson <url>
Import raw JSON from characterswithoutnumber.app and enable auto-sync.
/update
/sheet update
Instantly sync your active character stats with the stored source URL.
/link <url>
Set or change the sync source (JSON or Google Sheet) for your active character.
/sheet
View your active character. Use /sc for combat or /sf for full view.
/swn
/wwn
/cwn
Instantly generate a Level 1 character for the chosen system.
COMBAT & DICE
/wn-roll <exp> [msg]
Standard dice roller. Supports kh (Keep High) and kl (Keep Low) notation.
/3d6kh
/4d6kh
Quick specialized rolls that keep the highest 2 dice automatically.
/wn-attack [weapon]
Roll an attack using your character's sheet modifiers.
/wn-skill [name]
Perform a 2d6 skill check tied to your character stats.
/initiative
Roll and add yourself to the active initiative tracker.
GM TOOLS & MAPS
/tracker add <n> <h> <a>
Add a combatant to the tracker with Name, HP, and AC.
/tracker map
Opens the interactive web-based tactical map for token movement.
/importmap <image>
Upload a custom battlemap background for the tactical grid.
/gen <type>
Generate random planets, NPCs, corporations, or aliens.
/plot
/oracle
Generate random plot hooks or ask the 2d6 Oracle for a weighted response.
/reaction
/morale
Roll 2d6 NPC reactions or morale checks against a target score.
/weather
/encounter
/hazard
Quick checks for weather, random encounters, and environmental hazards.
CAMPAIGN & SETTINGS
/campaign join
Join an active campaign to sync your stats with the GM's tracker.
/language
Instantly toggle the bot's interface between 5 supported languages.
/starthere
Access the quick start guide for any of the supported game systems.
